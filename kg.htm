Early Steps
The JKG/KG faculty comprises of well qualified , creative and a dedicated team of teachers who are trained for the very purpose of pre-school education. Regular seminars are conducted to update the staff's knowldge in teaching methodology.
   The system is designed as to create a comfortable, cheerful and encouraging environment. Step-by-step structured curriculum is followed which without burdening the child, builds a strong foundation to ably handly the future detailed courses.
   Though various group and individual classactivities each child is given ample opportunity to enhance the personality traits, to add dimension to learning, to boost up the reasoning power and stimulate logical thinking ability. good manners and etiquettes are emphasised upon. Respect for elders and love for peers is inculated in the children.
Creative Activities
These are designed with the aim of learning with fun. Through teaching aids like blocks, abacus, puzzles, toys etc. The process of learning is made easy and interesting. The children are introduced to thematic poems, rhymes and songs and special emphasis is given to recitation and story - telling. Music and Dance activities comprise an integral part of the curriculum. The children are exposed to plethora of creative activities which include painting, colouring, drawing, printing, paper folding, tearing, pasting, collage making and plasticine modelling.
   These creative activities are conducted by well-qualified teachers who specialize in their respective fields.
Sports Activities
These activities are designed to enable child to be physically fit and to develop all the motor movements of the body. These includes in-door and out-door games with simple exercises, drills, fun races, football etc. complying with the child's interest. The Pre-primary wing has a well equipped toyroom where children have access to innumerable indoor games, puzzles, blocks and toys.
Cultural Activities
These are framed with the aim of entertainment to build stage confidence and inculate social values. Children are encouraged to speak on the mike and to perform on the stage. Inter-section competitons are held to instil a feeling of confidence and to discover and provide exposure to these budding talents. Fancy dress, art, writing, action-songs, elocution, singing, dance, games, collage making and plasticine modelling competitions are events to look forward to in which children participate with whole hearted enthusiasm. The parents of the participants are invited to witness their wards perform in the inter-section competitions held every month. This regular interaction enables and encourages the parents to be more actively involved in their child's progress in these extra-curricular activities
